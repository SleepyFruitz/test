Underseaged
Journey through post-apocalyptic water ruins in search for lost artifacts to rebuild your kingdom's only power source

High Concept

Brother and sister, Maisha and Kifo, are on an adventure through post-apocalyptic water ruins in search for lost artifacts to power and bring back technology to their civilization. They need to find how the old civilization collapsed and how they lived while fighting off buibuis which have infested the planet.

Game Play

Overview

Brother and sister, Maisha and Kifo, are on an adventure through post-apocalyptic water ruins in search for lost artifacts to power and bring back technology to their civilization. They need to find how the old civilization collapsed and how they lived while fighting off buibuis which have infested the planet.

characters

Maisha and Kifo

  • Maisha and Kifo are sister and brother and live in a nearby dying kingdom. It’s their job to explore the nearby ruins to gather the old technology to rebuild their civilization.
  • Maisha is a confident and headstrong. This is reflected in her game play style because she is a brawler and is not afraid to get in the fight.
  • Kifo is more timid and shy and takes a behind role and is a healer and acher.

    • Buibui

    • Buibuis are tricky mutant spider crabs that have infested the world

Player Objective

Primary Objective: Collect enough artifacts to rebuild and restore the power source.

    Learn To:

  • Move
  • Jump
  • Fight
  • Collect Artifacts
  • Heal

Level of Difficulty

  • Player must get past the enemies and then solve a puzzle in order to retrieve the artifacts.
  • As each level progresses, difficulty will increase. Enemies will be stronger and more difficult to defeat and the puzzles will become more complex.
  • Tutorial level will consist of learning how to control the character. Player will defeat the enemy which will drop the first artifact.
  • Player must collect 5 artifacts: betri, simu, mbali, tochi, and kontakt. The 5 artifacts will connect and combine into 1 main artifact that will be inserted to the power source.
  • Players can search through the ruins to find pearls so they are able to purchase upgraded armor for Maisha or stronger healing powers for Kifo.
  • If player loses a fight against a buibui they will lose 3 pearls.
  • Every time Kifo heals either himself or Maisha there will be a “cool down” period in which his healing powers needs to recharge.

Final Challenge

  • The final level will serve as the final test to the player’s ability to fight and solve puzzles. The final artifact will be guarded by Le King Crab.
  • The final puzzle will consist of both physical and mental abilities.
  • The player will need to make it through the room which has weak a floor. They must use any resources available in the room to jump over and avoid traps to get to the other side.
  • They will solve the ultimate intellectual puzzle to unlock the last artifact and then combine the 5 artifacts together.

Core Gameplay Mechanics

Maisha player character abilities

  • Jump
  • Fight
  • Collect Artifacts

Kifo player character abilities

  • Jump
  • Shoot long range bubbles bursts
  • Heal

Enemy character abilities

  • Attack

Controls

  • Right arrow to move forward
  • Left arrow to move back
  • Up arrow to jump
  • Left mouse click to attack/collect artifact
